Depth & Emotion

Esther Perel

The Challenge Preserve the emotional weight and dramatic pauses of the original speech without making it boring for a fast-paced feed.

The Execution Kept the original pacing. Applied warm cinematic color grading, gentle piano, and custom fade-in typography for emphasis. Zero jarring sound effects to maintain the intimate atmosphere.

The Result A thought-provoking, atmospheric video that stands out and stops the scroll through pure emotion, not cheap dopamine.
